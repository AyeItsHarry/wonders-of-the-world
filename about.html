New 7 Wonders of the world!

The new 7 wonders of the world was a campaign started in 2001 to choose new Wonders of the World. They were voted upon through both an online poll and phone voting. The winners were announced 6 years later on the 7th July 2007 in Lisbon.

However, the polling for this was considered unscientific due to people being able to cast multiple votes.
